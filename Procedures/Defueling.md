# **Defueling Checklist**
## Physical Fuel Ticket

- Fuel ticket must be filled out with 
- Date → Filled on Fuel Ticket
- Tail # → Filled on Fuel Ticket
- Company Name → Filled on Fuel Ticket
- Meter 1 → Filled on Fuel Ticket
- Meter 2 → Filled on Fuel Ticket
- Notify management with information on fuel ticket

## Lock-Out-Tag-Out (LOTO)
- Place LOTO on Fueling Truck
