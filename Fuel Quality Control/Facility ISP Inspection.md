# Fuel QC — Facility ISP / Daily QC Audit Checklist

**Version:** v1.0  
**Updated:** 2026-03-07  
**Duration:** 00:20

---

## Metadata

**Purpose**  
Perform a general-condition and safety audit of facility fuel equipment and support systems.

**Trigger**  
Daily facility QC / ISP review.

**Owner**  
Fuel QC / Supervisor

**Required Items**  
Facility access, inspection record, observation route, pen/device

**Inputs**  
ISP form, facility status, current equipment condition

**Outputs**  
Facility ISP completed and discrepancies logged

**Stop Conditions**  
Security breach, fire deficiency, unsafe hose or static equipment condition

---

## PREP

**Objective**  
Open the facility audit and establish the observation route.

**Checklist**

- [ ] ISP form Open
- [ ] Audit area Defined
- [ ] Facility access Verified

---

## CORE

**Objective**  
Inspect the listed facility condition items and record visible deficiencies.

**Checklist**

- [ ] General condition Checked
- [ ] Security Checked
- [ ] Fire deficiencies Checked
- [ ] Swivel hoses Checked
- [ ] Static reels Checked
- [ ] Fire extinguishers Checked
- [ ] Sump separators Checked
- [ ] Visible damage None
- [ ] Immediate hazards None

### DECISION: Was any safety or security deficiency found?

**YES**

- Escalate to supervisor
- RECORD: Log discrepancy
- Hold affected area / equipment as directed

**NO**

- Complete facility ISP

---

## CLOSEOUT

**Objective**  
Submit the facility condition audit with clear issue ownership.

**Checklist**

- [ ] ISP findings Logged
- [ ] RECORD: Discrepancies Logged / N/A
- [ ] Facility ISP Complete
